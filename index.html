Dr. Natsuki Yoshino

Researcher in Signal Processing, Violist

About Me

My research focuses on signal processing, particularly signal processing techniques applied to audio recordings of string instruments, and also includes research in statistical machine learning. I received Ph.D. from Hokkaido University.
